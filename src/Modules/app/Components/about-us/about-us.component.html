Digitizing Bookings
This WebApp is built to introduce Digital Bookings to Curtin University Campus Labs. The minimum viable product is deployed currently and will allow both students and admin (which includes Professors, Instructors and Management Staff) to book the CCNA lab easily through this WebApp. Students can easily book the lab for their learning purposes while the Admin can make changes if necessary by either Editing the current bookings or
By simply Deleting the Bookings, this results in automated emails to the affected parties. The vision of the WebApp is to incorporate Booking for all Labs on Campus and to eventually become a platform for students and faculty to check their respective university schedule.
